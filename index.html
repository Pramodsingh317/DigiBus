How It Works
1. Students submit route requests from the Student portal.
2. Go to Requests → assign fee.
3. Student pays and uploads a receipt. Go to Receipts → verify receipt.
4. Set bus card time limit and approve → student gets a downloadable bus card.
5. Bus Staff verify the card to confirm validity.
